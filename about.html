Who We Are

Rwanda's Dento-Medical Society

A nonprofit professional organization uniting dental and medical students and professionals to advance oral and public health across Rwanda and beyond.

Our Vision

Advancing Oral & Public Health Through Innovation and Partnership

We envision a Rwanda where oral health is recognized as foundational to general well-being — where every community, regardless of geography or income, has access to preventive care, modern clinical practice, and the research and education that drive both forward. Our work bridges dentistry and medicine, students and professionals, urban centres and rural districts, to make that vision a reality.

Our Story

Founded 2024 · Ngoma, Huye · Southern Province

In Rwanda, oral health has long existed at the margins of the national healthcare system — underfunded, underrepresented, and often invisible to the communities that need it most. Dental disease, though largely preventable, continues to affect children, adults, and vulnerable populations who lack access to even the most basic oral care.

RDMS was founded in 2024 to confront this reality head-on. Driven by a shared belief that oral health is inseparable from general health, our founders built a society that bridges the gap between dentistry and medicine — bringing professionals, students, and communities together under one mission.

Registered under RDB as a Non-Profit Limited by Guarantee (Code: 143885158), RDMS operates from Huye and reaches communities across Rwanda through field programs, research, education, and policy engagement.

Our Mission

“To unite dental surgeons, dental therapists, and public health professionals in advancing oral-systemic health through collaborative education, research, outreach, and policy advocacy.”
📍

Where We Operate

Headquartered in Ngoma, Huye (Southern Province), RDMS conducts programs across Rwanda — from urban clinics and university campuses to rural schools and underserved communities.

🤝

Who We Bring Together

Our members include dental surgeons, dental therapists, medical doctors, public health professionals, and students from across Rwanda's health sciences community — all united by one purpose.

🌍

Our Reach

While rooted in Rwanda, RDMS engages with global oral health networks, international awareness days, and cross-border partnerships — contributing to healthcare advancement beyond Rwanda's borders.

What Guides Us

Our Core Values

  1. 01

    Transparency & Accountability

    We operate with openness in all our activities — from governance and finance to research and community engagement. Trust is the foundation of everything we do.

  2. 02

    Teamwork & Collaboration

    Progress in oral health requires collective effort. We unite professionals across disciplines, institutions, and borders to achieve outcomes no single actor could accomplish alone.

  3. 03

    Selflessness & Compassion

    Our work is driven by genuine care for the communities we serve. We place patients and populations at the heart of every decision we make.

  4. 04

    Equity & Equality

    Quality oral healthcare is a right, not a privilege. We actively work to eliminate disparities — ensuring geography, income, or background never determines the care someone receives.

  5. 05

    Human Rights & Environment

    We uphold the dignity and rights of every individual we serve, and recognise that human health and environmental health are deeply interconnected.
