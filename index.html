Who We Are

Rwanda’s Dento-Medical Society

A nonprofit professional organization uniting dental and medical students and professionals to advance oral and public health across Rwanda and beyond.

Our Vision

Advancing Oral & Public Health Through Innovation and Partnership

We envision a Rwanda where oral health is recognized as foundational to general well-being — where every community, regardless of geography or income, has access to preventive care, modern clinical practice, and the research and education that drive both forward. Our work bridges dentistry and medicine, students and professionals, urban centres and rural districts, to make that vision a reality.

Our Story

Founded 2024 · Ngoma, Huye · Southern Province

In Rwanda, oral health has long existed at the margins of the national healthcare system — underfunded, underrepresented, and often invisible to the communities that need it most. Dental disease, though largely preventable, continues to affect children, adults, and vulnerable populations who lack access to even the most basic oral care.

RDMS was founded in 2024 to confront this reality head-on. Driven by a shared belief that oral health is inseparable from general health, our founders built a society that bridges the gap between dentistry and medicine — bringing professionals, students, and communities together under one mission.

Registered under RDB as a Non-Profit Limited by Guarantee (Code: 143885158), RDMS operates from Huye and reaches communities across Rwanda through field programs, research, education, and policy engagement.

What We Do

Our Core Services

From school screenings and preventive programs to research and professional education — RDMS delivers integrated oral health services across Rwanda.

School-Based Dental Screenings

Healthy teeth start young. RDMS conducts systematic dental screenings at primary and secondary schools across Rwanda, identifying untreated caries, gum problems, and developmental issues before they become serious. Our teams visit schools with portable equipment, assess students, and connect those who need follow-up care with local dental services. We also provide on-the-spot oral hygiene education to students and teachers alike — making schools a frontline of oral health prevention.

Preventive Care Programs

Prevention is more powerful — and more affordable — than treatment. RDMS runs structured preventive care programs that reach communities through multiple channels: daily oral health messaging, fluoride application sessions, oral hygiene kits distribution, and community workshops on brushing, diet, and dental habits. Our preventive programs are designed for schools, health centers, and community groups — with content tailored to both children and adults.

Oral Health Research & Data Collection

Evidence drives change. RDMS conducts and supports oral health research across Rwanda — collecting data on disease prevalence, care access, and health behaviors to build the evidence base needed for policy reform. We collaborate with academic institutions, hospitals, and international research bodies to generate studies that are both locally relevant and globally significant.

Dental Medicine Chronicles

Knowledge should be shared. The Dental Medicine Chronicles is RDMS’s regular publication series — covering clinical case studies, public health updates, policy commentary, and innovations in dental medicine. Written by and for dental and medical professionals in Rwanda, the Chronicles bridge the gap between academic research and everyday clinical practice.

RDMS Research Academy

Continuous learning is the backbone of a strong health system. The RDMS Research Academy is a structured educational platform providing dental and health professionals in Rwanda with access to curated research lessons, case-based discussions, and continuing professional development content. Topics range from oral-systemic health links and preventive dentistry to emerging clinical techniques and public health policy.

Community Mobile Clinics

Access to dental care should not depend on where you live. RDMS deploys mobile dental clinics into underserved rural and peri-urban communities across Rwanda — bringing professional oral healthcare directly to people who might otherwise never see a dentist. Services include free dental examinations, extractions, fluoride treatment, and distribution of oral hygiene supplies.

Partner with RDMS

We work with government institutions, NGOs, dental associations, and international partners to scale our impact across Rwanda.
